Our story

Nils and Abby met through a fortuitous programming loophole. Abby saw a kind, handsome, and interesting individual on a preview on a dating app, it was Nils of course. But sadly the free version of the app would not let a message be sent to Nils. Abby found through a series of steps in the dating app she could gain an infinite amount of free points, enough to message Nils. Messages were sent, critiques and appreciation of campus art installations were exchanged. Nils invited Abby in November of 2017 to a wonderful dinner in Ann Arbor. They have been enjoying eating and cooking together since and believe in the importance of love over algorithms.
