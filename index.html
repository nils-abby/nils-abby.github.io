Our story

Even though they worked across the street from each other for years, Nils and Abby needed the internet to finally meet. In late 2017, Abby found a preview of Nils' profile but was asked for payment before anything else could be revealed. Instead of bowing to corporate pressure, Abby found an infinite-likes marketing loophole and got Nils' contact info for free. Messages were sent, critiques of campus art installations were exchanged, and Nils invited Abby to a wonderful dinner in Ann Arbor. They have been enjoying eating and cooking together since and believe in the importance of love over algorithms.
