COVID Precautions

We are thankful for your willingness to travel at this time. While we do have a small guest list we will have folks coming in from different professions and exposure levels. Please read the following before arriving in preparation.

Venue

To the extent possible we will be focusing on holding events outside. The wedding ceremony and reception will occur outside.

Vaccination + Masks

Everyone attending this wedding is vaccinated. Some towns in the Bay Area (e.g. Berkeley, San Francisco) do require proof of vaccination for certain activities. The Bay Area is currently not requiring masks for indoor activities at this time.

  • Santa Clara county status

Testing

We are providing at home self tests for all guests to take before attending any wedding events. Upon arrival message Abby and Nils to pick up your test. Please take your test several hours before attending your first wedding event (e.g. rehersal, dinner, ceremony). If you test positive we will set up a streaming alternative for your participation and bring you tasty wedding food.
