Travel Reccomendations

Accomodations

For family members we reccomend coordinating to arrange shared Airbnbs / VRBOs near the Rose Garden neighborhood in San Jose (see email we have sent out to family for more details).

For single and/or budget travelers we recommend the Signature Inn near Santa Clara University which offers a retro motel vibe nearby one of our favorite coffee shops.

Please reach out with questions or concerns.

Transportation

For immediate family members we reccomend coordinating between family groups to arrange a shared rental car, especially if you are coming in early. For single travelers rideshare apps (Uber/Lyft) and public transportation are good options especially if flying into SJC. We are also planning on arranging for a transportation service (to and back) from the wedding itself.

Airport

The easiest airport to fly into is Norman Y. Mineta San Jose International Airport (SJC). However, Oakland (OAK) and San Francisco (SFO) might offer better rates for flights and are only an hour drive away.
